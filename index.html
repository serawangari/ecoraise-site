About Leafscapes

We channel corporate CSR into tangible environmental action by sponsoring tree-planting projects led by schools and local communities.

  • Corporate partners co-create measurable outcomes and reportable impact.
  • Schools & nurseries supply locally adapted seedlings and environmental education.
  • Communities plant, care for, and monitor trees for long-term stewardship.

Programs
