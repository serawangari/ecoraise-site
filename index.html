About Leafscapes

Leafscapes is a community-driven platform that connects corporates, schools, and local communities to restore landscapes through tree planting and monitoring. We partner with schools that host tree nurseries, enabling corporates to source seedlings, track their survival through spatially-enabled dashboards, and report real impact to stakeholders.

By blending technology, education, and environmental stewardship, Leafscapes ensures CSR initiatives go beyond planting — they nurture communities, ecosystems, and the future.
